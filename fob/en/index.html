  • concept

    fob is a boutique pocket watch and accessories brand created in Paris in 2012.

    Born from the singular vision of a young team inspired by the heritage of this iconic and since neglected timepiece, fob reinvents the pocket watch.

    Drawing a standard of quality from traditional watchmaking expertise and a simple elegance from the fundamentals of design, fob creates unique and modern pocket watches.

    All fob timepieces are designed and assembled in Parisian watchmaking workshops by expert craftsmen.

    fob watches are complemented by a collection of interchangeable Masks — watch back covers — in a variety of colors, materials and textures, allowing you to build a unique watch that evolves throughout the seasons.

    Subtle yet impertinent, fob rings in a new era for the pocket watch, elevating it yet again to a treasured object for men and women to be worn around the neck, or in your jacket or pants pocket.

  • origins

    As the predominant timepiece in men‘s and women‘s pockets for over four centuries, the pocket watch was, beyond its primary function, a cherished object for its owner.

    After it fell out of circulation with the rise of the wristwatch, fob aims to bring this legendary object back, with a new generation of watches built on an alliance of heritage and modernism.

    fob watches distinguish themselves by their exceptional craftsmanship uniting elegance, reliability and creativity.

    Designed and assembled in Paris, they follow the standards of French watchmaking tradition and know-how.

    Beyond their reliability, fob adds a unique and innovative concept to their watches: the opportunity to personalize your pocket watch with a collection of interchangeable Masks — watch back covers — in a variety of colors, materials and textures.

    With geometric curves of art deco inspiration, The Rehab is the first collection by fob. Its sleek and streamlined design rings in a new era for the pocket watch, as a coveted, subtle and impertinent fashion accessory.

    the rehab

    The Rehab, fob‘s first collection, pays homage to the timeless elegance of the pocket watch.

    With an art deco feel, sleek lines and elegant sobriety, The Rehab plays with the contrast of eras and is made using singular and noble materials.

    The Rehab is a remarkable pocket watch. It integrates an automatic mechanical movement, renowned for its quality and precision.

    fob Masks — interchangeable watch backs — personalize and add a touch of unexpected impertinence to your watch.

    The Rehab is a unique take on the pocket watch: a fetish object with an unprecedented design.

  • know-how

    fob watches aim for excellence.

    All of fob‘s Masks are handcrafted by a prestigious Parisian leatherworker, who selects for fob only materials of an exceptional quality.

    To make every Rehab watch unique and adaptable, fob presents several lines of interchangeable Masks.

    Classic Edition

    The four classic and timeless Masks are made from dyed leather, personalizing The Rehab with elegance and subtleness.
    Masks tinted Amethyst, Sapphire, Malachite and Onyx metamorphosize The Rehab for every desire and fantasy.

    Order

    Limited Edition

    Shagreen, Sheepskin, Gator or vintage Havana leather — the materials and textures of our limited edition Masks further explore the concept of unexpected contrasts and push the limits of classic design norms in luxury timepieces, making The Rehab a unique and audacious fashion accessory.

  • collaborations

    fob reinvents the pocket watch, in a singular, modern and artistic universe.

    Thanks to its masks concept, fob gives an area of freedom to artists, adhering to the brands universe and wishing to enrich it with their own influences and inspirations.

  • fob artists

    NASTY

    Nasty first discovered the graffiti art in the late 90’s. Pioneer, he contributes to introduce this New-York born art in Paris. His singular style, with simple letters and acidic colors, made him famous. The Parisian subway, with its train, its enameled plates or its maps, quickly became his favorite playground. Today, it is the same spirit, on the same materials, that he exposes his work in the art galleries.

    For fob, Nasty reinvents The Rehab in a very urban spirit, sticking, in the choice of shapes and colors, to his very own style. Each Mask of the Nasty limited edition is part of a 50 pieces exclusive artwork, made in Paris streets, using paint spray.
    More about Nasty


    THOMAS V

    Thomas V is a young artist and jeweller, designing and hand-working its jewels from his Parisian workshop.
    Sensitive to shapes and playing on superimpositions, Thomas V cuts rough materials, gold, brass, silver or even chirurgical metal, to create simple, elegant and enigmatic pieces.

    For his first collaboration with fob, Thomas V transforms the watch The Rehab, working the brass, to create a watch with a strong character, rough and elegant.
